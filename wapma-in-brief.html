WAPMA In Brief

The West African Pharmaceutical Manufacturers association was founded in 2005 and officially launched in 2007 at Accra, Ghana by His Excellency Aliu Mahama, Vice President of the Republic of Ghana. The first elected President of WAPMA, Mazi Sam Ohuabunwa, was on hand for the occasion, which was recognized as part of the celebration of the 50th independence anniversary of the Republic of Ghana.

WAPMA's current membership comprises 30 member companies of the Pharmaceutical Manufacturers Association of Ghana (PMAG), 100 member companies of the Pharmaceutical Manufacturers Group of the Manufacturers Association of Nigeria (PMG-MAN) and one company from Cote d'Ivoire.
