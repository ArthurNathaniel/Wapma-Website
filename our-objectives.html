Our Objectives

WAPMA's main objective is to represent and support the common business, scientific and technical interests of its members namely, but not limited to the generic medicines pharmaceutical industry and relevant and/or related associations. These Interests shall not be restricted to the West African region but shall also include relations globally and in so doing The Association seeks to:



  • Promote access to safe, quality and efficacious generic medicines to patients.

  • Facilitate economic growth and development of a sustainable and viable pharmaceutical industry.

  • Strengthen the local production of generic medicines in SADC; and Act as an industry coordinating body by promoting excellence, facilitating networking opportunities, disseminating information and raising awareness regarding pharmaceutical related matters

ADVOCACY

WAPMA promotes investments and creates knowledge based jobs, thereby improving competitiveness as well as public health.

DRUG RESEARCH

WAPMA is an association of regional pharmaceutical associations committed to the development of Africa-based pharmaceutical manufacturers.

MANUFACTURING

WAPMA is committed to setting and achieving high standards for prioritized skills sets within the industry. WAPMA is committed to ensuring that personnel with the relevant skills are available.
