Welcome to
WAPMA

WAPMA represents the pharmaceutical manufacturing companies and associations across the globe. The pharmaceutical manufacturing industrys develop and provide medicines and vaccines that improve the life of patients worldwide.

Our Vision

To establish a modern and sustainable pharmaceutical manufacturing industry in West Africa, which enables people to have equal and early access to the best and safest medicines,

Our Mission

To promote pharmaceutical manufacturing in West Africa, by creating a favourable economic, regulatory and political environment, that enables the pharmaceutical manufacturing industry...

Our Commitment

WAPMA is committed to working with stakeholders and taking up the challenge of being the private sector voice in addressing challenges that hinder the development and growth of the pharmaceutical industry.

THE PHARMACEUTICAL INDUSTRY

WAPMA is committed to implementing activities and programmes that will strengthen the regional pharmaceutical manufactures associations.

WAPMA is committed to setting and achieving high standards for prioritized skills sets within the industry. WAPMA is committed to ensuring that personnel with the relevant skills are available to meet the needs of the industry.

ADVOCACY

Our Objectives

WAPMA's main objective is to represent and support the common business, scientific and technical interests of its members namely, but not limited to the generic medicines pharmaceutical industry and relevant and/or related associations.
